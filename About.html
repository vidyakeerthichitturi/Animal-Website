ABOUT THIS WEBSITE

Purpose of the website

The purpose of this website is to provide information about different types of animals such as wild animals, domestic animals, birds, aquatic animals, and reptiles. The website helps users understand animal habitats, characteristics, and importance in the ecosystem. This website also aims to create awareness about wildlife conservation and the need to protect endangered species. By learning about animals, users can develop respect for nature and understand the role animals play in maintaining ecological balance. The website is designed for students and animal lovers to explore animal categories in a simple and informative way.
